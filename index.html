Nigeria

Nigeria is a country in West Africa. It is the most populous nation in Africa, with a population exceeding 200 million. Nigeria's most popular city, Lagos, has a population of over 20 million. Nigeria is ethnically diverse, with a couple hundred ethnic groups. It is home to Africa's largest entertainment industry, with its music genre "Afrobeats", which has worldwide popularity and has become one of the fastest-growing music genres globally with grammy winning artists.
